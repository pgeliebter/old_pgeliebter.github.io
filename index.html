Why I became a software developer

I always loved technology! Ever since I was a kid computery stuff came natural to me. I loved tinkering (and breaking) all sorts of things from changing config files in Diablo to get more gold through adding mods for Tribes 2.

Another thing I love is to make processes easier for people. I am always thinking about improvement that can be made pretty much everywhere.

Way back yonder at the beginning of Covid I worked with a dev team in order to move our current business processes online. It was a grueling yet extrememly satisfying project. Getting a database up for 200+ therapists and integrating it with legacy systems.

After working for 3+ years at Comprehensive I discovered that technology is what makes businesses run.
